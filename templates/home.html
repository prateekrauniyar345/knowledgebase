Under the Hood: Our Robust Architecture

Built on battle-tested AI and cloud technologies, KnowledgeBase is designed for performance, security, and scalability. Here’s a glimpse:

  • **Vector Store:** Chroma for blazing-fast similarity search.
  • **Relational Database:** Postgres for robust metadata management and versioning.
  • **AI Engines:** Leveraging OpenAI GPT-4o, Mistral, and more for advanced RAG and natural language processing.
  • **Asynchronous Processing:** Celery/RQ for seamless background ingestion and embedding.
  • **Secure & Scalable:** Designed with AWS IAM, VPC, and robust security protocols.
